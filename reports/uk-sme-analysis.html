3
Fix Qualification at Demo Request Stage
Owner: Kirsty Freely + VP Sales · Timeline: Immediate · Impact: Medium-High
Conversion fix
  • 50% of UK losses are "No Decision" = demos given to clinics that were never going to buy.
  • 33% "Stayed with Provider" = demos given to clinics still contracted to their current system.
  • Implement a 3-question qualification gate before scheduling UK demos: (1) What is their current software? (2) Have they decided to switch? (3) What is their go-live date?
  • Any clinic that can't answer (2) and (3) should be put into a nurture sequence, not a demo.
  • Expected outcome: reduce UK CL from 12/month to 5–7/month, improving win rate to 30%+.
